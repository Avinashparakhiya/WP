## 📈 Top 3 ASO Optimization Strategy Guidelines

1. **Optimize Visual Assets (Screenshots & App Icon)**:
   - Visuals drive 60% of conversion. Build high-contrast, labeled screenshots showcasing the three key features: (1) Direct Chat, (2) Status Saver, and (3) AI Assistant cards.
   - Use clean device frames (iOS/Android) with short callout text overlays like *"Direct Chat (No Contact Saved)"*.

2. **Localization**:
   - Translate the Title, Subtitle, Short Description, and Keywords list into regions with high search volume (e.g. Hindi/India, Spanish/Latin America, Portuguese/Brazil) to expand organic reach by up to 100%.

3. **Reviews and Ratings Drive Ranking**:
   - Prompt users to rate the app inside the app using `StoreKit` or native popups after they successfully save their 3rd status or send a direct chat. Positive ratings significantly boost app search algorithms.
